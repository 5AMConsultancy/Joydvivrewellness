Wellness Courses

Somatic Wellness Workshops

Somatic wellness is the practice of reconnecting mind and body — learning to listen to what your body is telling you, releasing stored tension, and moving with awareness and intention.

Each workshop gently weaves together practices that calm the nervous system, restore mobility and reawaken the connection between how you feel and how you move.

Workshops are run in different areas of South Africa, and online internationally. Contact Joy D'Vivre Wellness via email, WhatsApp or social media to find a workshop that suits you.
